Report dishonest benefit claims

Use this service to anonymously report someone you think is dishonestly claiming benefits.

If you give enough information, the Department for Work and Pensions will investigate and may contact the person.

Action will only be taken if the person has committed benefit fraud.

What you will need

To report someone you’ll need to:

  • give details to help identify the person
  • answer questions about what they’re doing wrong
